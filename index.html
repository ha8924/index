About us

Italia specializes in beautifully crafted individual wood fired pizzas and Italian-inspired dishes. At Italia, our recipe for success is simple – Great food & care makes customers return every time.

Fresh, Flavorful and Wood Fired

Italia uses the most flavorful tomatoes, cheeses and other fresh locally-sourced ingredients to make our wood fired pizzas and Italian-inspired dishes. Our pizza dough is also made with “secret” ingredients that are hand worked and tossed to ensure that our pizzas have a deliciously thin crispy crust, with a chewy center, topped with the freshest ingredients. They are then fired to perfection in our wood fired oven to seal in flavor and transform our menu items into fine crafted masterpieces. Our pastas, appetizers, salads, gelato, and local craft beers are just a few more reasons to enjoy a meal at Italia.

A welcome refuge from the bustle of the city, Italia, as it’s affectionately called – offers a full range of flavourful, authentic Italian dishes and is known as one of the best restaurants in Saigon’s prestigious District 1.
Helmed by Chef de Cuisine Enrico De Martino, the menu dabbles in regional favourites from north to south – from Wagyu osso buco to home-made pasta crafted with imported Italian flour.
The contemporary dining room exudes a distinctively exuberant flair, with walls of vibrant Italian mosaics, elaborate candelabras and a collection of differently styled dining chairs – a creative nod to the 40 distinctive corners of Italy.
